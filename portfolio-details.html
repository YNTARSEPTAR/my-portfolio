Vegetation Change Analysis

  • Technology Used: Remote Sensing and GIS
  • Project date: May 2023

Brief Information

In this project, I employed satellite image processing using ERDAS Imagine, along with Remote Sensing (RS) and Geographic Information Systems (GIS), to analyze the 10-year changes in vegetation, built-up areas, and water bodies. By harnessing the capabilities of satellite image processing, we gain a deeper understanding of urbanization's ecological implications. This integrated approach enables us to make informed decisions and devise effective strategies for sustainable urban planning and environmental conservation.
